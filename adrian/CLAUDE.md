## Game Concept / Concepto del Juego

**English:** A 2-player browser-based maze racing game with combat. A multiplayer battle
royale + crafting + maze escape where players must find the exit of a dangerous labyrinth,
battle other players with weapons, build traps and defenses from crafted materials, survive
environmental hazards, and be the first to escape OR the last one standing.

**Español:** Un juego de laberinto para 2 jugadores en el navegador con combate. Batalla
real multijugador + crafteo + escape de laberinto: encontrar la salida de un laberinto
peligroso, luchar con armas, construir trampas y defensas, sobrevivir peligros del entorno,
y ser el primero en escapar O el último en pie.

**Target Audience:** Kids ages 6-12, families playing together.

**Controls:** Player 1 = Arrow Keys, Player 2 = WASD. Goal: first to the exit wins! 🏆
